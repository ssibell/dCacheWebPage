Göttingen dCache Workshop March 16-17, 2011

This year, the German Support dCache workshop was hosted by the II. Institue of Physics of the Göttingen University. The event was sponsored by the HGF "Physics at the Terascale" and the "European Middleware Initiative".

dCacheToGo : The Virtual Box dCache Image

The Virtual Box image can be used in the Migration and WebDAV Hands-On sections.
